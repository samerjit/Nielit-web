About NIELIT Imphal

NIELIT Imphal, established in 1989 under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India, is a premier technical institute located at Akampat, about 5 km from Imphal city. Spread over 20 acres, the campus houses modern infrastructure, including smart classrooms, computer and electronics labs, a digital library, seminar halls, mechanical workshops, hostels, and recreational facilities.

The institute offers formal courses such as Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), BCA, MCA, and MSc IT, affiliated with Manipur University. Non-formal courses include NIELIT ‘O’ Level, and certifications in AI, Big Data, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, and more. NIELIT Imphal promotes digital literacy, skill development, and provides scholarships for SC/ST/PH/female students, contributing to the technological growth of Northeast India. Read More

Accreditation

National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bangalore, accredited NIELIT Imphal with a B+ grade on 10th February 2007, making it the first institute in Manipur assessed by NAAC before its 4th year. In the 2nd Cycle of Accreditation, NAAC awarded a B Grade on June 25, 2015.
